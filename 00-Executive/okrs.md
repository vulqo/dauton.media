## How we use OKRs

Quarterly objectives, numerical key results. Review at end of each quarter, reset for next. Objectives describe the outcome we want; key results are the measurable evidence that we got there.

Scoring: 0.0 to 1.0 per key result. 0.7 is the target — if we consistently hit 1.0, the OKRs aren't ambitious enough.

## Yearly meta-objectives (evaluated in March 2027)

These are the objectives that carry through the whole year and define whether Year 1 succeeded:

- **Recognition:** Culture Wiki is cited by at least one major Latin music publication as a source.
- **Authority:** At least 300 domains link to Culture Wiki organically.
- **Completeness:** Full graph exists for 200+ Venezuelan artists and their collaborators.
- **Economics:** The project covers its operational costs through revenue.
- **Independence:** No single source, partner, or outside voice can dictate editorial direction.
